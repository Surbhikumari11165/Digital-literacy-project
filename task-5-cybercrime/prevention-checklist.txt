Stay Safe Online – Prevention Guide

 General Safety Tips:
Create strong passwords using letters, numbers, and symbols
Turn on two-step verification for better security
Do not open unknown links or download suspicious files
Avoid sharing private details on public platforms
Always think before posting anything online
Stay away from online arguments or harmful content
Report and block accounts that seem suspicious or abusive
Be careful while chatting with strangers on the internet

 Financial / UPI Safety:
Never disclose your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details to anyone
Double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment
Do not accept unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps

 Report Cybercrime:
Visit: cybercrime.gov.in
Call: 1930 (24x7 helpline)
